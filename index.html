About Us


ChangeMakers Refugee Forum is a rights-based, non-government organisation (NGO) representing 15 refugee-background communities in Wellington, New Zealand.

We work towards our vision of New Zealanders from refugee backgrounds participating fully in New Zealand life.

Underpinning our work is a rights-based approach and the Standards for Engagement.
